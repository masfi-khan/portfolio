MEET MASFI KHAN

Masfi Khan grew up in Queens, New York. She currently attends Queens High School for the Sciences at York College. Despite her school being STEM-centric, Masfi is passionate about writing poetry in order to uncover the layers of herself and the ever-changing world. She enjoys being founder and editor-in-chief of For the Sonorous, a literary journal for women and non-binary people of color. This summer, Masfi is thrilled about participating in the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program, where she exposes herself to computer science and joins a close-knit sisterhood. She believes that technology is integral to our society's modernization and that women need to have a growing input in its direction. She likes singing along off-key to songs, intersectional feminism, and really great books.
